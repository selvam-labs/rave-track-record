Methodology

RAVE is a fully automated paper-trading engine: a locked, deterministic rule set — status-timed entries, a VIX-regime position governor that caps size per volatility band, a 325-session trend gate, hard stop-losses, and a portfolio drawdown circuit breaker. No discretion, no manual overrides, no ML. It runs unattended on AWS Lambda every trading day at 10:00 AM ET against an Alpaca paper account and commits its results here.
